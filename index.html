2-minute missed-setup protocol
1
Close the chart for a moment
Stop replaying the miss in real time. That gives you enough distance to document it instead of spiraling.
2
Capture the setup screenshot
Save the chart before it changes. The picture will be far more useful later than a vague memory.
3
Write what was valid
Describe the setup, level, trigger, and context. Focus on facts first, emotions second.
4
Name the blocker
Fear, hesitation, distraction, or drawdown pressure. One honest sentence is enough.
5
Leave one instruction for next time
Turn the lesson into a rule, trigger, or checklist item so future-you has a clear plan.
